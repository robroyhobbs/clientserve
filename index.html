First 30 days

Four weeks. One goal: find the strongest ICP and message before scaling anything.

Step 1
Extract what you know
  • 90-min founder session: wins, losses, objections, best segments
  • Audit the 30-company research for patterns and unproven bets
  • Review pipeline, proof assets, and current page messaging
ICP ranking · differentiators · proof gaps · scorecard
Step 2
Lock the wedge
  • Pick one primary ICP
  • Revise homepage around one outcome
  • Align ICP pages to: problem → outcome → proof → CTA
  • Point content toward 3 assets: proof piece, comparison, how-it-works
Unified message · core assets · paid-readiness gate
Step 3
Test in market
  • Run one ICP landing page test
  • Launch founder-led outbound into the chosen segment
  • Insert proof assets into the live funnel
Early signal on message, segment, and objections
Step 4
Double down or adjust
  • Review what pulled — cut what didn't
  • Decide whether paid scales now or waits
  • Build the 60-day plan from real data
Clear next bet · channel priority · team ownership

Immediate priorities

The five things that matter most in the first two weeks.

1
Pick one ICP

Stop marketing to five verticals at once. Choose the segment that converts fastest and retains best.

2
Fix the homepage story

"AI-enabled customer service" is too generic. Lead with a measurable business outcome for the chosen ICP.

3
Build trust assets

There's no visible proof on the site today. One case study and one comparison piece change the conversion dynamic.

4
Gate paid spend

Don't run ads until the landing page message is validated through outbound or organic signal first.

5
Clean up brand signals

Same meta description on every page. Social tags still point to @lovable_dev. Small fixes, big credibility impact.
